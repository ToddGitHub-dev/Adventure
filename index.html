Tom and Jerry

Once upon a time there lived two happy friends, Tom and Jerry. Tom was a cat and Jerry was a mouse. Tom, with his masculinity, had a responsibility to provide security whilst Jerry, with his brain, made decisions. Not until they faced El Nino. Every food that they had was gone and now they were` only left with themselves. The next thing that comes in their minds was to hunt each other. Unfortunately, luck was not in favour of Jerry. Tom with his strength, had an advantage over Jerry.

However, they both agreed to come up with all possible ideas that they could. The ideas that they formulated were:

  • Go in their own ways looking for food
  • Hunt each other

"Hunt each other it is", cried Tom.

Jerry was nervous about Tom’s intension. He was about to protest but he realised that it was too late. Tom had his claws out of his paws already, jumping like a giant beast towards Jerry. After some seconds, Jerry realised that he was running away from a monster, once was a friend. With his clever tricks, he fitted himself in a niche on a wooden wall that Tom couldn’t reach and Tom and Jerry were never to be friends again ever after.
